Hi There,

My name's Ivan. I am a full stack software developer in training with a background in computer hardware. Ever since I can remember, I've been fascinated with technology. My love for video gaming as a child led me to become interested in building a gaming PC, and by winter of '07 I had built my first machine. Since then, and until now, my professional career had revolved around computer hardware. With a hunger for knowledge and a lack of a clear path forward in hardware, I decided to work towards a career in software development. This has turned out to be one of the best decisions I have ever made. My journey into software development has reignited my sense of purpose, and I couldn't be more excited to continue to learn and expand my toolbox. Check out a few of my projects below!
